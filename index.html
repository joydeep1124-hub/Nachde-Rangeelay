Who We Are

Rooted in Tradition.
Alive with Energy.

Nachde Rangeelay Academy trains the next generation in the folk roots of Bhangra and Gidha — keeping it authentic, keeping it exciting, and keeping it modern. Whether you're stepping onto the dance floor for the first time or refining your technique, you'll find your home here.

From weddings to competitions, our dancers bring unmatched energy wherever they go.
